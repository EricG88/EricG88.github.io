Pre-Purchase Inspection


Momentum offers Pre-Purchase Inspections to help you make the right choice when selecting your next vehicle.
We complete a very comprehensive visual inspection for cosmetic, corrosion and mechanical condition.

We even measure the paint thickness down to the difference of one micron and record the results for you.
This will tell you if the car has ever been painted (which can indicate a collision repair in its past).
Armed with our detailed checklist, you will be in a better negotiating position with the seller.

The price for this service is $249 and includes a detailed report of the vehicle's condition.
